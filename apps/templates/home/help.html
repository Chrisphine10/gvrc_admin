GVRC Admin Help Center

About GVRC Admin

GVRC Admin is a centralized directory and administrative system for managing community-based facilities including health facilities, police stations, CBOs, safe houses, legal aid centers, gender desks, and other community organizations.

Community Facility Types

Health Facilities

Manage healthcare facilities and medical services:

  • Hospitals, Clinics, Dispensaries
  • Health Centers, Laboratories, Pharmacies
  • Medical staff and equipment tracking
  • Health service availability monitoring
Security & Police Facilities

Track police stations and security services:

  • Police Stations and Police Posts
  • Security Offices and Personnel
  • Emergency response coordination
  • Security equipment and resources
Community Organizations

Manage community-based organizations and support services:

  • Community-Based Organizations (CBOs)
  • Non-Governmental Organizations (NGOs)
  • Faith-Based Organizations
  • Community Centers and Youth Centers
Support & Protection Services

Track support and protection facilities:

  • Safe Houses and Shelters
  • Legal Aid Centers
  • Gender Desks and Women Centers
  • Counseling and Rehabilitation Centers

System Modules

Community Facilities Management

Manage all types of community-based facilities:

  • Add and update facility profiles
  • Track facility types and ownership
  • Monitor facility status and compliance
  • Manage contact information and operating hours
Services & Programs

Track services offered by community facilities:

  • Health, Security, and Legal Services
  • Counseling and Protection Services
  • Education, Training, and Advocacy
  • Emergency and Referral Services
Human Resources

Manage staff across all facility types:

  • Medical, Security, and Legal Staff
  • Counselors, Social Workers, Psychologists
  • Administrative and Support Personnel
  • Volunteers and Community Workers
Operational Equipment

Manage operational equipment and emergency vehicles:

  • Ambulances and Emergency Vehicles
  • Medical and Diagnostic Equipment
  • Communication and Security Equipment
  • Maintenance and Operational Tracking

Data Entry Guidelines

Best Practices:
  • Always verify facility registration numbers for accuracy
  • Keep contact information and emergency contacts up to date
  • Regularly update service availability status
  • Maintain accurate staff records and qualifications
  • Document infrastructure changes and maintenance
  • Track target populations and operating hours
